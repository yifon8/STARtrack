
Attempt 2 — Basic Structure (~1 minute spoken)

At my previous job, I was on a cross-functional project team and one of my peers — a developer — kept delivering their pieces late. This created a bottleneck for the rest of us.

Since I had no authority over them, I requested a one-on-one conversation. I came prepared with data showing how their delays were pushing back our shared milestones. I made sure to frame it around our common goal rather than pointing blame.

After that conversation, they started flagging blockers earlier and we got back on track. The project delivered on time and the working relationship stayed positive.
