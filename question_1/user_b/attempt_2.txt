At my last job I was on a cross-functional product launch team. One of the engineers kept missing internal deadlines by a few days, and it was creating a ripple effect on the rest of us. I had no authority over them, so I set up a one-on-one to talk through it.

I showed them how the delays were affecting the overall timeline and asked what was getting in the way. Turned out they were being pulled into support work by another team. We agreed on some changes — protected work hours and earlier communication when something was going to slip. After that the project got back on track and we launched on time.
